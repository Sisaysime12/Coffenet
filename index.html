Our Story

Established in 2018, CoffeeNet Trading Plc is a grassroots company established by Kebere Negash and Eyob Tesfaye whom through the years has been processing washed and natural coffees in Uraga, Guji Zone.

Born and raised in Uraga, Kebere is one of the prominent entrepreneur who is aspiring to impact the lives of his community through the school project that he have built in the Layo Teraga village.

He is also mainly known for constructing wet mills and roads for the Coffee Cooperatives as well as communities at large.

Philosophy

CoffeeNet runs its business activities with the ultimate objective to discover high quality coffee and develop a meaningful relationship with the farmers.

Our company is currently playing its own role in the country’s economy and with our coffee supplier community. We are a part of our community and at coffeenet we believe in sharing and paying it forward to the land that has given so much to us all.

We give back to our community each year with our Social Contribution Program. We are dedicated to supporting the farmers and their families. This includes building projects like schools, roads, supplies and other infrastructure.
